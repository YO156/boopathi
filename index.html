FIRST GOLD MEDAL

Dr. R. Deena, born on 18-10-2002 in Salem, Tamil Nadu, India, has created a new world record.
He achieved this by carving the names of fourteen presidents into pencil lead within 2 hours,
setting a new world record.

SECOND GOLD MEDAL

The record for carving out the names of the maximum number of sculptures on pencil leads was set by Dr. R. Deena (born on October 18, 2002) of Salem, Tamil Nadu.
He carved out the names of 13 famous sculptures, namely:
David, Thinker, Venus de Milo, The Kiss, Moses, Discobolus, Cloud Gate, Dying Gaul, Moai, Motherland, Maman, Henry Moore, and Pieta
on 13 pencil leads at the age of 19 years, 10 months, and 26 days, as confirmed on September 13, 2022.
